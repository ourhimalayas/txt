Young Kim (U.S. representative for California’s 39th congressional district)
杨·金（美国加利福尼亚州第 39 国会选区代表）
 
I rise and strong support of this bipartisan bicameral effort to help restore Taiwan’s observer status at the World Health Organization and World Health Assembly
我站在这里大力支持这项两党两院的努力，以帮助恢复台湾在世界卫生组织和世界卫生大会的观察员地位
 
I am proud to have introduced the House version of this bill which has nearly 130 bipartisan cosponsors
我很自豪地介绍了该法案的众议院版本，该法案有近130个两党共同提案人
 
Time and Time again Taiwan has proven to be a trusted ally of the United States and to be a world leader in the global health security
台湾一次又一次被证明是美国值得信赖的盟友，并在全球卫生安全领域处于世界领先地位
 
From 2009 until 2017, Taiwan had observer status at WHO and WHA, and was revoked due to the pressure from the Chinese government
2009年至2017年，台湾在WHO和WHA的观察员地位，在中共政府的压力下被撤销
 
This bill will direct the Secretary of State to implement a strategy to restore Taiwan’s observer status
该法案将指示国务卿实施恢复台湾观察员地位的战略
 
And I am hopeful that today we can send a united message to the people of Taiwan that we value their input and that give them a seat at the table benefits all of us
希望今天我们能够向台湾人民发出一个统一的信息，我们重视他们的意见，让他们在谈判桌前有一个席位，我们所有人都会对此受益
 
Support for Taiwan is more important than ever
支持台湾比任何时候都重要
 
We all know today that as Russia invades Ukraine，China is eyeing Taiwan
今天我们都知道，随着俄罗斯入侵乌克兰，中国正盯着台湾
 
A threat to freedom anywhere is a threat to freedom everywhere
对任何一个地方自由的威胁就是对所有地方自由的威胁
 
That is why I urge my colleagues to support this effort and give the people of Taiwan a voice, and vital information sharing at World Health Organization and World Health Assembly
这就是为什么我敦促我的同事们支持这项努力，让台湾人民有发言权，并在世界卫生组织和世界卫生大会上分享重要信息
 
I am proud to lead this effort and will continue to be an unwavering voice for global human rights and freedom.
我很自豪能够领导这项工作，并将继续坚定不移地为全球人权和自由发声
