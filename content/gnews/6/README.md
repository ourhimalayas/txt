## 欧盟委员会主席宣布他们正在提议对俄罗斯实施第五轮制裁
` 洛杉矶盘古农场 HIMALAYA LA PANGU` [轉載自GNews](https://gnews.org/zh-hans/2314624/)

Ursula von der Leyen (President of the European Commission)
乌苏拉·冯德莱恩（欧盟委员会主席）

We all saw the gruesome pictures from Bucha and other areas
我们都看到了布查和其他地区的可怕照片

from which Russian troops have recently left
俄罗斯军队最近离开的地方

Yesterday I conveyed to President Zelenskyy my condolences
昨天我向泽连斯基总统转达了我的哀悼

and assured him of the European Commission’s full support in these terrible times
并向他保证欧盟委员会将在这些可怕的时期给予全力支持

These atrocities cannot and will not be left unanswered
这些暴行不能也不会被置之不理

The perpetrators of these heinous crime crimes must not go unpunished
这些滔天罪行的肇事者绝不能逍遥法外

The European Union has set up a joint investigation team with Ukraine
欧盟与乌克兰已成立了联合调查组

Its task is to collect evidence and investigate war crimes
它的任务是收集证据和调查战争罪行

and crimes against humanity in Ukraine
和在乌克兰的反人类罪

Russia is waging a cruel and ruthless war
俄罗斯正在发动一场残酷无情的战争

not only against Ukraine’s brave troops
不仅针对乌克兰英勇的军队

but also against its civilian population
但也针对其平民

It is important to sustain at most pressure on Putin and the Russian government at this crucial point
对普京和俄罗斯政府在这个关键时刻施加最大的压力是很重要的

The full packages of sanctions have hit hard
全套的制裁措施已经重创了

and limited the Kremlin’s political and economic options
并限制了克里姆林宫的政治和经济选择

we’re seeing tangible results
我们看到了切实的成果

But clearly, in view of events, we need to increase our pressure further
但显然，鉴于事态发展，我们需要进一步加大压力

So today, we are proposing to take our sanctions a step further
所以今天，我们提议将制裁更进一步

We will make them broader and sharper
我们将使它们更宽广和更锐利

so that they cut even deeper into the Russian economy
以便他们更深入地切入俄罗斯经济

This fifth package has six pillars
这第五个包裹有六个支柱

First, we will impose an import ban on coal from Russia with 4 billion euros per year
首先，我们将对俄罗斯的煤炭实施每年40亿欧元的进口禁令

This will cut another important revenue source for Russia
这将削减俄罗斯的另一个重要收入来源

Second, a full transaction bank on four key Russian banks
第二、俄罗斯四大银行的全交易银行

among them VTB, the second largest Russian bank
其中包括俄罗斯第二大银行 VTB

these four banks, which we now totally cut off from the markets
这四家银行现在被完全切断了与市场的联系

we present 23% of market share in the Russian banking sector
欧盟的金融业务占有俄罗斯银行23%的市场份额

This will further weakened Russia’s financial system
这将进一步削弱俄罗斯的金融体系

Third, a ban on Russian vessels and Russian operated vessels from accessing EU ports
第三、禁止俄罗斯船舶和俄罗斯经营的船舶进入欧盟港口

certain exemptions will cover essentials
某些豁免将涵盖必需品

such as agriculture and food products humanitarian aid as well as energy
例如农业和食品人道主义援助以及能源

Additionally, we will propose a ban on Russian and Belarusian road transport operators
此外，我们将提议禁止俄罗斯和白俄罗斯的公路运输运营商

This ban will drastically limit the options for Russian industry to obtain key goods
该禁令将极大地限制俄罗斯工业获得关键商品的选择

Forth, further targeted export bans worth 10 billion euros in areas in which Russia is vulnerable
第四，在俄罗斯脆弱地区进一步实施价值100亿欧元的出口禁令

This includes, for example, quantum computers and advanced semiconductors
这包括，例如，量子计算机和先进的半导体

but also sensitive machinery and transportation equipment
还有敏感的机械和运输设备

With this we will continue to degrade Russia’s technological base and industrial capacity
这样一来，我们将继续削弱俄罗斯的技术基础和工业能力

Fifth, specific new import bans worth 5.5 billion euros
第五、价值55亿欧元的具体新进口禁令

to cut the money stream of Russia and its oligarchs
将切断俄罗斯及其寡头的资金流

on products from wild salmon to seafood to liquor
包括从野生三文鱼到海鲜再到酒等
