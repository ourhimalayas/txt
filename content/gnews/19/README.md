**中共蹂躏世界****  ****牺牲的是海外华人的形象、安全和利益**

**The Price of the CCP Ravaging the World Will Be Borne by Overseas Chinese**

我要跟大家要讲的今天更重要的事情，就是从前天到现在国际形势，大家都看到发生了什么样的变化？最最大的变化就是在欧洲、日本，甚至在东欧这些国家，美国、加拿大这就不用说了，像南美就反应的比较慢一点，这些国家都在谈一个问题——海外中国人。

A very important thing that I want to tell you today is what big changes we have seen in the world since the day before yesterday. The biggest change is that countries in Europe, Japan and even Eastern Europe, not to mention the United States and Canada, as well as South America, though it is a little slow, all these countries are talking about one issue – overseas Chinese.

海外中国人这个话题现在谈得真的是太猛了，无非是以上几点，就是海外华人过去这八年到十年，那种骄傲，什么“我的祖国”、“共产党是天下无敌”， 然后到处骂每个国家，给全世界开药方。海外华人协会，以及开着法拉利跑车到处那种轰啦啦轰啦啦，然后海航的买买买，王健林的这种傲慢，马云的到全世界去不可一世的俯视全球。

The topic of overseas Chinese is really starting to heat up, which includes no more than the several reasons that I mentioned earlier. The overseas Chinese over the past eight to ten years have been so arrogant, talking about stuff like “my motherland” and “the Chinese Communist Party is invincible”, and going around scolding every country and trying to prescribe solutions to the whole world. Also there are many overseas Chinese associations, people driving Ferrari sports cars and making noises everywhere, and then there is HNA’s making acquisitions everywhere, Wang Jianlin’s arrogance, and Jack Ma’s roaming around the world and looking down upon the whole world.

中国企业家一骂就是全人类都是笨蛋，真的把世界几乎过去十年蹂躏在地上，想怎么蹂怎么蹂，这种蹂躏的情况的代价是什么？——海外华人的形象、海外华人的安全和海外华人的利益。我不知道大家意识到了没有，这个后果相当严重。

Chinese entrepreneurs have scolded the whole mankind to be stupid and ravaged the world on the ground as much as they want to for almost a decade. What is the cost of this? The image of overseas Chinese, the safety of overseas Chinese and the interests of overseas Chinese. I don’t know if people have realized this or not. The consequences are very serious!

官方数字600万，实际在海外[华人]750万，这不包含香港、台湾，这是多大的基数啊！这些人过去和国内共产党、海外华人协会，还有什么福建会、这会那会、统战会、战略什么协会、国际关系会，这些会真的是很有可能被西方世界一锅给烩了，列为你是共产党的代言人——非法代言人。

The official number of overseas Chinese is 6 million, and the actual number is 7.5 millions, excluding Hong Kong and Taiwan. What big a number it is! People used to work with the CCP in China, with overseas Chinese associations, the so-called Fujian Associations, this and that association, the United Front Work Association, strategy association and international relations association. These associations are really likely to be braised by the Western world in one pot all together and all labeled as the agents of the CCP – the illegal ones.

更夸张的事情是，海外的这些上学的这些孩子们是我们最最担心的。毫不夸张地说，中国人想未来在几百年想再回到以前真的是很难了。

The situation of the kids who are studying overseas are the ones we worry about the most. It is no exaggeration to say that it’s going to be really hard for Chinese to go back to the old days in the next few hundred years.

西方世界敞开了胸怀接纳中国人，友好地，甚至不但带着笑容，甚至还带着一种崇敬的心情。中国人带着两个大 TITLE 来了，第一个中国人的文化——灿烂的几千年的文明文化，外国人确实喜欢；另外一个——中国巨大无比的市场，外国人更喜欢。

The Western world accepted the Chinese with an open heart, very friendly, with a smile on their face, and even with a sense of reverence. The Chinese came with two big titles: the first one is the Chinese culture – a splendid civilization of thousands of years, which foreigners really liked; the other one – the huge Chinese market, which the foreigners liked even more.

现在市场变成了威胁和武器，中国人的文化剩下了傲慢，全是共产主义。西方世界在审视新的中国人的时候，已经跟原来完全不一样了。

But now the market has turned to a threat and a weapon, and what’s left in the Chinese culture is just arrogance and communism.  The Western world is looking at the new Chinese in a completely different way than it did before.

我给你们举例说明，两件事儿你就可以看得出来。刚刚的世界上的所谓的教育机构，最近都在也是视频会议，多个会议，所谓世界教育合作组织、各大院校合作组织，在开会当中谈得最多的话题——到底有多少你们学校的学生是中国共产党的家人？到底有多少中国来的学生，他会是共产党派出来的？有多少中国学生是对我们是危险的？一旦是西方和中共开战，这些学生会是什么样的一个结果？

I’ll give you two examples, and you can tell that yourself. Just recently, educational institutions in the world participated in teleconferences, multiple conferences organized by the so-called international education associations and associations of major colleges and universities. And the topics that they talked about the most are: how many of your school’s students are family members of the CCP? How many Chinese students were sent by the CCP? How many Chinese students are dangerous to us? What will happen to these students if the West goes to war with the CCP?

这个话题跟二战前对待的日本，以及当时伊拉克、利比亚这样的小国家，比那还夸张。世界教育各部门对这件事情开始审视的时候，大家去想想吧。

They went more overboard with these topics than how Japanese people were treated during WWII, and how small countries like Iraq and Libya were treated previously. When the education sector in the world starts to look into this matter, you can go think about it.
